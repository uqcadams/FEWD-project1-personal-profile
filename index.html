Greetings, I’m Chris.

I'm currently based out of Brisbane, Australia.

Background

I’m a challenge-driven young professional who has sought adventure and opportunity around the world. Working in fields as diverse as education, scuba diving, eco-tourism, and politics, I have a firm track record of tackling new experiences with enthusiasm and vigor.

My adventurous drive has taken me around the world. I've lived and worked in Australia, Italy, the UK, Malaysia, and New York. Separately, I've explored South-East Asia, Africa, and Europe as a solo, independent backpacker. I crave the unknown, I thrive in foreign and unusual environments, and I never shy from making new friends and acquaintances along the way.

Highlights:

  • Living in New York as an Advisor to Former Prime Minister of Australia, the Hon. Kevin Rudd
  • Completing my Divemaster certification alongside the Bajau Laut "Sea Nomads" on a remote island in Malaysian Borneo
  • Travelling overland across Africa with just a backpack and camera in hand
